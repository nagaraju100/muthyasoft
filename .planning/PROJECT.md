# Data Engineering Tutoring Platform

## What This Is

A professional tutoring landing page for Nagaraju Gajula, a Manager-level Data Engineer with 10+ years of enterprise experience at companies like LatentView Analytics, YASKAWA, Goldman Sachs, and Texas Instruments. The platform offers 1-on-1 personalized data engineering tutoring covering Python, PySpark, Databricks, Airflow, and cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure). Targeting serious learners from beginners to mid-level professionals who want to master modern data engineering through industry-tested knowledge and practical mentorship.

## Core Value

Students can connect with an experienced industry practitioner who brings real production experience, deep technical expertise, and a proven teaching approach to accelerate their data engineering journey.
